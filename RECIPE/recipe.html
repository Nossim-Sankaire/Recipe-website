Baked chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbs Soy sauce
  • 1 tsp Chicken masala
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • 1 tsp Cayenne
  • 1 tsp chilli
  • 1 tsp parsley
  • 3 Tbs Vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp Black pepper
  • Drum-sticks
  • Salt

Method

Place the drum-sticks in a bowl and wash. Dry the drum-sticks using a paper towel. Add the soy sauce,chicken masala,cayenne,chilli,black pepper,Vegetable oil,parsley and sprinlke some salt. Grate the garlic and ginger in the bowl then mix. Once done cover the drum-sticks using a cling film and refrigirate. Let it marinate for 1hour. Cook it in the oven for 45 minutes at 180.

Pineapple smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 Pineapple
  • 500 ml water
  • 6 Tbs sugar

Method

Wash the pineaplle. Peel the pineapple and cut it into small pieces. Boil the pineapple peels in water. Drain the water and place it in the blender together with the chopped pineapples.Add sugar and blend.Sieve to remove the pulp and refrigerate. Serve when cold.

Coconut rice

Ingredients

  • Coconut milk
  • rice
  • 1 Tbs salt
  • 2 Tbs vegetable oil
  • Enough water
  • Chopped onions

Method

Fry the onions in vegetable oil till golden brown. Add water and cover untill it boils. Add the rice and cover. Once the water significantly reduces, add coconut milk. cover the rice for 15-20 mins for the rice to dry.
